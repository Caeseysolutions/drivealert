Emergency SOS — the feature that sets it apart

This is what makes the 622GW unique. If the vehicle detects a severe impact and the driver doesn't respond, the device automatically sends an emergency alert containing:

  • Precise location via What3words (accurate to 3 square metres)
  • Driver's medical information
  • Vehicle details
  • Video footage from the last few minutes
Proven in practice: Nextbase has submitted over 146,000 dashcam videos to UK police forces. 70% led to further police or legal action. The Emergency SOS has demonstrably helped in real emergency situations.

Image quality in real use

The 4K resolution makes a genuine difference — number plates are readable at distance and at speed. This is crucial after an accident or hit and run. The image stabilisation produces smooth footage even on UK B-roads and motorways.

Are dashcams legal in the UK?

Yes. Dashcams are completely legal in the UK, Ireland, Belgium, Netherlands and across Europe. The UK's National Dash Cam Safety Portal (created by Nextbase) allows motorists to submit footage directly to police forces. The BGH ruling in Germany and similar legal frameworks across Europe allow dashcam footage as evidence under certain conditions.
